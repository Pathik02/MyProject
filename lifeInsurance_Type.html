Types of Insurance

Provides a financial benefit to the beneficiaries in case of the insured person's death.

These are essential insurance policies you may require throughout your lifetime.

Child insurance plans secure the financial future of your children by providing funds for their education and other needs.

Marriage insurance plans provide financial support to ensure a grand and hassle-free wedding.

Retirement insurance plans help you build a substantial retirement corpus, ensuring financial independence in your golden years.
Health Insurance

Covers medical expenses incurred due to illness or injury, including hospitalization, surgeries, and prescription drugs.

Auto Insurance

Protects against financial loss in case of accidents, theft, or damage to vehicles.

Homeowners Insurance

Covers damage or loss to a home and its contents, as well as liability for accidents that occur on the property.

Renters Insurance

Protects renters against loss or damage to personal belongings and provides liability coverage.

Disability Insurance

Provides income replacement in case of disability that prevents the insured from working.

Travel Insurance

Covers unforeseen events during travel, such as trip cancellation, medical emergencies, and lost luggage.

Short-Term Insurance

Provides temporary coverage for fixed or adjustable risks, such as bridge loans, house repairs, or emergency funding.
